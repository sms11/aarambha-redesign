How closely should we match Premier's design?

Premier uses a warm, refined, editorial aesthetic — navy + gold, serif typography, generous whitespace, full-width hero banners. Your current Aarambha site is more playful with teal/orange/purple and bouncy animations.

A

Near-Clone

Same color palette (navy + gold), similar layout structure, similar typography. Basically Premier's design language applied to Aarambha's content.

B

Inspired By

Adopt their level of polish and refinement (clean layouts, elegant typography, warm institutional feel), but keep Aarambha's own color identity and brand personality.

C

Cherry-Pick Elements

There are specific things you like about Premier (e.g., their hero section, their card layout, their navigation) and you want those, but the rest can stay different.
