Homepage Section Structure

Should we mirror Premier's homepage flow or adapt it for Aarambha?

Premier's Homepage Flow

  1. Hero banner — "GREATNESS BEGINS HERE" with rotated text, full-width image
  2. "A Place to Belong" — short intro paragraph
  3. "A Nurturing Space" — two-column: image + text
  4. Programs grid — 5 program cards (Early Years → High School)
  5. Extracurricular life — 3 featured areas with background imagery
  6. "A Glimpse of Excellence" — student achievements
  7. Knowledge Partners — scrolling logo carousel
  8. Parent Testimonial — quote carousel
  9. CTA — "Ready to begin your child's journey?"
  10. Footer — 5-column layout
A

Mirror Premier's Structure

Use the same section order and layout patterns. Replace their content with Aarambha's (programs, stats, testimonials, etc.).

B

Adapt for Aarambha

Use Premier's visual style but reorganize sections to better fit Aarambha's content — e.g., keep the stats section, adjust programs to match Aarambha's 4 levels (Early Years → High School), skip the partners carousel if not needed.
